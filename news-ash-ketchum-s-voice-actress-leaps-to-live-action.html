Ash Ketchum's Voice Actress Leaps to Live Action

Sarah Natochenny breaks barriers after 19 years

IndieWire
November 30, 2025
3 min read

Imagine being the voice behind a beloved character for nearly two decades, only to face uncertainty about your next career step. This is the story of Sarah Natochenny, the talented voice actress who has brought Ash Ketchum to life in the English dub of 'Pokémon' for 19 years. According to IndieWire, Natochenny has finally made the leap to live-action, joining the set of Tim Heidecker's 'Raccoon.' This milestone marks a significant turning point in her career, one that has been shaped by her determination to overcome industry obstacles.

Natochenny's journey as a voice actress began with her iconic role as Ash Ketchum, a character that has become synonymous with the 'Pokémon' franchise. Her success in this role has been a testament to her talent and dedication, but it also presented its own set of challenges. As a voice actress, Natochenny has had to navigate the complexities of the voice-over industry, where opportunities can be limited and competition is fierce. Despite these hurdles, she has managed to build a successful career, leveraging social media and conventions to connect with fans and stay relevant in the industry.

The voice-over industry is one that is often overlooked, yet it plays a vital role in bringing characters to life and enhancing the overall viewing experience. Voice actors like Natochenny are the backbone of this industry, using their talents to create memorable characters and stories. As the industry continues to evolve, with new technologies and platforms emerging, voice actors must be adaptable and willing to take risks. Natochenny's transition to live-action is a prime example of this, demonstrating that voice actors can successfully transition to other areas of the entertainment industry. This not only opens up new opportunities for voice talent but also highlights the importance of versatility and resilience in the face of an ever-changing industry.

Natochenny's story is not just one of personal achievement; it also has a broader impact on the entertainment industry and beyond. Her success serves as an inspiration to aspiring voice actors and actresses, showing that with hard work and determination, they too can achieve their goals. Moreover, her transition to live-action helps to break down barriers between different areas of the entertainment industry, demonstrating that voice actors can excel in a variety of roles. As the entertainment industry continues to evolve, stories like Natochenny's will become increasingly important, highlighting the need for greater diversity and representation in all areas of the industry.

In conclusion, Sarah Natochenny's leap to live-action is a significant milestone, not just for her own career but also for the voice-over industry as a whole. As the industry continues to grow and evolve, it is essential that voice actors are recognized for their talents and given the opportunities they deserve. Natochenny's story is a powerful reminder of the importance of perseverance and adaptability, and it serves as an inspiration to voice actors and actresses around the world. According to IndieWire, Natochenny's journey is a testament to the power of hard work and determination, and it will undoubtedly continue to inspire and motivate others in the years to come.
