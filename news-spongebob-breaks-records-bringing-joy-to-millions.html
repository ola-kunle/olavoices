SpongeBob Breaks Records, Bringing Joy to Millions

New movie shatters 10-year record, a win for voice-over artists

ComicBook.com
December 19, 2025
3 min read

Imagine a world where a sponge who lives in a pineapple under the sea can bring people together, evoke tears of joy, and create a sense of nostalgia that transcends generations. This is the world of SpongeBob SquarePants, and it's just achieved a remarkable milestone. According to ComicBook.com, the new SpongeBob SquarePants release, The SpongeBob Movie: Search for SquarePants, has broken a 10-year-old record, hitting theaters this weekend and captivating audiences worldwide.

The latest film brings back Tom Kenny to voice SpongeBob, with Mark Hamill also joining the cast. This record-breaking achievement is a testament to the enduring power of the SpongeBob franchise, which has been entertaining fans since its debut in 1999. The show's creator, Stephen Hillenburg, may have passed away in 2018, but his legacy lives on through the beloved characters he brought to life. The new movie's success is also a reflection of the dedication and talent of the voice-over artists who have been instrumental in shaping the franchise's sound and personality.

The voice-over industry plays a vital role in bringing animated characters to life, and the success of The SpongeBob Movie: Search for SquarePants is a shining example of the impact that talented voice-over artists can have on a project. Voice actors like Tom Kenny, who has been voicing SpongeBob for over two decades, have become an integral part of the franchise's identity. Their ability to convey emotion, humor, and depth through their voices has helped to create a sense of connection with audiences that is hard to achieve in live-action films. The voice-over industry is often overlooked, but it's an essential component of the entertainment industry, providing opportunities for talented artists to showcase their skills and bring joy to millions of people around the world.

The success of The SpongeBob Movie: Search for SquarePants matters because it reminds us of the power of imagination, creativity, and storytelling. In a world where division and negativity often dominate the headlines, SpongeBob's message of friendship, optimism, and inclusivity is a much-needed breath of fresh air. The film's record-breaking achievement is also a testament to the enduring appeal of animation and the voice-over industry, which continues to evolve and innovate with each new project. As the entertainment industry continues to shift and change, the voice-over industry is poised to play an increasingly important role in shaping the sound and personality of future films and TV shows.

In conclusion, the success of The SpongeBob Movie: Search for SquarePants is a celebration of the voice-over industry and the talented artists who bring animated characters to life. As we look to the future, it's clear that the voice-over industry will continue to play a vital role in shaping the entertainment landscape. Whether you're a fan of SpongeBob or just a lover of animation, this record-breaking achievement is a reminder of the power of imagination and creativity to bring people together and inspire us to be our best selves.
