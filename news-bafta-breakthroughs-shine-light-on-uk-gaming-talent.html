GamesIndustry.biz
November 25, 2025
3 min read

Imagine being part of a prestigious group of creatives, recognized for your innovative work in the gaming industry. For the 2025 UK BAFTA Breakthroughs, this dream has become a reality. According to GamesIndustry.biz, the games cohort is made up of talented individuals, including Kyle Banks, director of Farewell North, and Sally Beaumont, lead voice actor and script consultant for Old Skies. These emerging voices are pushing the boundaries of storytelling and immersion in the gaming world.

The BAFTA Breakthroughs initiative is designed to identify and support the next generation of British talent in the film, television, and gaming industries.
