Voice Actor Jim Ward Passes Away at 66

Legendary voice of Captain Qwark in Ratchet & Clank

Kotaku
December 11, 2025
3 min read

The world of voice acting has lost a shining star with the passing of Jim Ward, the iconic voice behind Ratchet & Clank's beloved Captain Qwark, at the age of 66. According to Kotaku, Ward's untimely death has sent shockwaves through the gaming and voice-over communities, leaving fans and fellow voice actors reeling in grief. Ward's impressive body of work spanned multiple franchises, including Metal Gear Solid 3, Grim Fandango, and F.E.A.R., cementing his status as a versatile and talented voice actor.

Jim Ward's legacy extends far beyond his impressive resume, however. His voice brought joy and laughter to countless gamers around the world, and his portrayal of Captain Qwark remains an unforgettable part of gaming history. As a radio host and voice actor, Ward's passion and dedication to his craft inspired a new generation of voice-over artists, and his influence will continue to be felt for years to come.

The voice-over industry is a close-knit community, and Ward's passing serves as a poignant reminder of the impact that voice actors have on our lives. As the demand for high-quality voice-over work continues to grow, Ward's legacy highlights the importance of talented voice actors who can bring characters to life. The voice-over industry is not just about providing a voice for a character; it's about creating an emotional connection with the audience, and Ward's work exemplified this. His passing creates an opportunity for emerging voice talent to step up and fill the void, but it also underscores the challenges of finding unique and talented voices that can leave a lasting impression on audiences.

Ward's death is a significant loss, not just for the voice-over community, but for the gaming industry as a whole. His contributions to the world of gaming have left an indelible mark, and his memory will continue to be celebrated through the characters he brought to life. As we mourn the loss of this talented voice actor, we are reminded of the power of voice-over work to touch our hearts, evoke emotions, and create lasting memories.

In conclusion, Jim Ward's passing is a stark reminder of the significance of voice actors in our lives. As we reflect on his remarkable career and the characters he brought to life, we are inspired to appreciate the talented individuals who work tirelessly behind the scenes to create immersive experiences for us. The voice-over industry has lost a true legend, but Ward's legacy will continue to inspire and influence a new generation of voice actors, ensuring that his memory lives on through the characters he loved and the audiences he touched.
