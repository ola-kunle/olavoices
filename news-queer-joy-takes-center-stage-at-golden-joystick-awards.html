Queer Joy Takes Center Stage at Golden Joystick Awards

Jennifer English's Emotional Speech Sparks Hope and Inclusion

Thepinknews.com
November 21, 2025
3 min read

Imagine being part of a community where your identity is not only accepted, but celebrated. This is the powerful message that resonated with many as Jennifer English, the talented voice actor behind Clair Obscur: Expedition 33, took the stage to accept her Golden Joystick Award for Best Lead Performer. Her emotional speech, dedicated to 'queer joy,' sent a ripple of hope and inclusivity throughout the gaming and voice-over community.

According to Thepinknews.com, English expressed her shock and gratitude upon receiving the award, using her platform to amplify the importance of representation and acceptance. In a world where LGBTQ+ individuals often face marginalization and exclusion, English's words served as a beacon of light, reminding us that everyone deserves to be seen and heard. The Golden Joystick Awards, a prestigious event that recognizes excellence in the gaming industry, provided the perfect backdrop for English's poignant speech.

The voice-over industry, in particular, has a unique opportunity to promote diversity and inclusivity. As voice actors, they have the power to bring characters to life, shaping the way we perceive and interact with the world around us. English's win and speech highlight the significance of authentic representation in gaming and beyond. By embracing queer joy and promoting acceptance, the voice-over community can help create a more compassionate and empathetic society.

The impact of English's speech extends far beyond the gaming industry. It has the potential to inspire a new generation of voice actors, writers, and content creators to prioritize inclusivity and diversity in their work. As the voice-over industry continues to evolve, it is essential that we recognize the value of diverse perspectives and experiences. By doing so, we can create a more vibrant and dynamic landscape of characters, stories, and worlds that reflect the complexity and beauty of human experience.

This moment matters because it reminds us that our words and actions have the power to shape the world around us. In an industry where representation and inclusivity are often lacking, English's speech serves as a powerful reminder of the importance of empathy, compassion, and understanding. As we move forward, it is crucial that we continue to amplify the voices of marginalized communities, promoting a culture of acceptance and celebration.

In conclusion, Jennifer English's Golden Joystick Award win and speech are a testament to the transformative power of queer joy and inclusivity. As we reflect on the significance of this moment, we are reminded that the voice-over industry has a unique opportunity to shape the narrative of our collective humanity. By embracing diversity, promoting acceptance, and celebrating our differences, we can create a brighter, more compassionate future for all.
