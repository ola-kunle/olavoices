Coca-Cola's AI Holiday Ad Disaster Sparks Debate

70,000 prompts later, a disappointing result

Boredpanda.com
November 19, 2025
3 min read

Imagine a holiday season without the warmth of a well-crafted advertisement to get you in the mood. For many, Coca-Cola's holiday commercials have been a staple of the season, evoking feelings of nostalgia and joy. However, this year's attempt at creating a festive ad using artificial intelligence has fallen flat, leaving many to wonder if AI is truly the future of content creation.

According to Boredpanda.com, Coca-Cola's latest holiday commercial, generated using AI, took a staggering 70,000 prompts to produce. The result, unfortunately, has been met with widespread criticism, with many calling it a half-hearted attempt at capturing the spirit of the season. This debacle has sparked a heated debate about the role of AI in content creation and the potential consequences for human creatives, particularly in the voice-over industry.

The voice-over industry, in particular, has a lot at stake in this debate. As AI-generated content becomes more prevalent, voice-over professionals are left to wonder if their skills will soon be replaced by machines. The idea that a machine can replicate the emotion, nuance, and depth that a human voice can bring to a project is a daunting one. However, this also presents an opportunity for voice-over artists to showcase their unique talents and the value they bring to a project. By highlighting the importance of human emotion and connection in content creation, voice-over professionals can demonstrate that their skills are still essential in the age of AI.

This issue goes beyond the voice-over industry, however. The implications of AI-generated content on our cultural landscape are far-reaching. As we become increasingly reliant on machines to create our entertainment, advertising, and even news, we risk losing the heart and soul that makes content meaningful. The holiday season, in particular, is a time when we come together to connect with loved ones and experience the magic of the season. If we allow AI to take over the creative process, we risk losing the emotional resonance that makes this time of year so special.

As we move forward in this era of AI-generated content, it's essential to consider the impact on human creatives and the cultural significance of the content we consume. While AI may be able to replicate certain aspects of human creativity, it lacks the emotional depth and nuance that makes content truly memorable. By recognizing the value of human voice and emotion in content creation, we can ensure that the stories we tell continue to resonate with audiences and inspire meaningful connections.

In conclusion, Coca-Cola's failed attempt at creating a holiday ad using AI serves as a reminder of the importance of human creativity and emotion in content creation. As the voice-over industry navigates this new landscape, it's crucial to highlight the unique value that human voice-over professionals bring to a project. By doing so, we can ensure that the stories we tell continue to touch hearts, strike nerves, and evoke reactions, rather than simply being a product of machine-generated content.
