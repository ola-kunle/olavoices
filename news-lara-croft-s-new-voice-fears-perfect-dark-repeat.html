Lara Croft's New Voice Fears Perfect Dark Repeat

Tomb Raider's voice actor shares concerns about game's success

Kotaku
December 19, 2025
4 min read

Imagine being the new voice of an iconic character like Lara Croft, only to worry that your performance might be overshadowed by the ghost of a past project. This is the reality for the star of the new Tomb Raider games, who is dreading a repeat of Perfect Dark, a game that has left a lasting impact on the gaming industry. According to Kotaku, the voice actor behind Lara Croft is anxious about the game's success, fearing that it might not live up to the expectations of fans and critics alike.

The Tomb Raider series has been a staple of the gaming world for decades, with Lara Croft being one of the most recognizable characters in the industry. The new games, which are currently in development, promise to bring a fresh perspective to the series, with a new voice actor taking on the role of Lara Croft. However, the voice actor's concerns about a repeat of Perfect Dark are not unfounded. Perfect Dark was a game that received widespread critical acclaim, but its success was also marred by controversy and delays. The game's development was plagued by issues, and the final product was not what fans had expected.

The voice-over industry is also watching the development of the new Tomb Raider games with great interest. The success or failure of the game could have a significant impact on the industry, particularly for voice actors who are looking to break into the gaming world. According to industry experts, the voice-over industry is becoming increasingly competitive, with more and more actors vying for a limited number of roles. The new Tomb Raider games could provide a much-needed boost to the industry, creating new opportunities for voice actors and providing a platform for them to showcase their talents.

The connection to the voice-over industry is clear: the success of the new Tomb Raider games could have a ripple effect on the industry as a whole. If the game is successful, it could lead to more opportunities for voice actors, as well as increased recognition and respect for the profession. On the other hand, if the game fails to deliver, it could have a negative impact on the industry, making it harder for voice actors to find work and gain recognition. As the voice actor behind Lara Croft so aptly put it, the fear of a repeat of Perfect Dark is a very real concern, not just for the gaming industry, but for the voice-over industry as a whole.

So, why does this matter? The answer is simple: the success or failure of the new Tomb Raider games has the potential to impact not just the gaming industry, but the voice-over industry as well. As we wait with bated breath to see how the new Tomb Raider games will fare, one thing is clear: the voice-over industry is watching, and the outcome could have a lasting impact on the profession.

In conclusion, the new voice of Lara Croft is not just worried about the success of the game; she is worried about the impact it could have on the voice-over industry as a whole. As we look to the future of the industry, it is clear that the success or failure of the new Tomb Raider games will be a defining moment, one that could have a lasting impact on the profession.
