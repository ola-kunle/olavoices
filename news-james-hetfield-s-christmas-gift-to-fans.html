James Hetfield's Christmas Gift to Fans

Metallica's lead vocalist shares a classic tale

Loudwire
December 19, 2025
3 min read

As the festive season unfolds, a surprise gift from Metallica's James Hetfield has warmed the hearts of fans worldwide. According to Loudwire, the iconic musician has shared a spoken word narration of the beloved Christmas tale, 'A Visit From St. Nicholas,' commonly known as 'Twas the Night Before Christmas.' This heartwarming gesture not only showcases Hetfield's versatility as an artist but also highlights the timeless appeal of this classic story.

The narration, which has been made available to fans as an early Christmas treat, is a beautiful rendition of the poem, first published anonymously in 1823. The story has since become an integral part of Christmas tradition, with its opening line, 'Twas the night before Christmas,' being one of the most recognizable in literature. Hetfield's deep, resonant voice brings a new level of depth and emotion to the narrative, making it a truly special listening experience.
