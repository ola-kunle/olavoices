Liam Hemsworth Wins Over Geralt's Voice Actor

Doug Cockle praises Hemsworth's performance as Geralt

PC Gamer
November 20, 2025
3 min read

Imagine being in the shoes of Liam Hemsworth, taking on the iconic role of Geralt in the Netflix series The Witcher, only to be met with lukewarm reception. The pressure to deliver is immense, especially when you're following in the footsteps of a beloved character. According to PC Gamer, Doug Cockle, the voice actor behind Geralt in CD Projekt's videogame trilogy, has come to Hemsworth's defense, stating that he 'holds up pretty well' as the character. This endorsement is a significant vote of confidence, especially considering the devoted fan base of The Witcher series.

For those unfamiliar with the series, The Witcher follows the story of Geralt, a monster hunter with supernatural abilities, as he navigates a treacherous world filled with magic and mythical creatures. The character has been portrayed by Henry Cavill in the previous seasons, but Hemsworth took over the role in the fourth season. While some fans have been skeptical about the change, Cockle's praise is a reassuring sign that Hemsworth has done justice to the character. Cockle's comments suggest that Hemsworth has successfully made the character his own, which is no easy feat given the intense scrutiny and loyalty of the fan base.

The voice-over industry is also closely watching this development, as it highlights the challenges and opportunities that come with bringing a character to life. Voice actors like Cockle have spent years perfecting their craft, and their performances can make or break a character. The fact that Cockle is willing to publicly support Hemsworth's portrayal of Geralt speaks to the camaraderie and respect that exists among voice-over professionals. This story also underscores the importance of voice-over work in content creation, as a talented voice actor can elevate a character and bring them to life in a way that resonates with audiences. For voice-over professionals, this story serves as a reminder that their work is crucial to the success of a project, and that their performances can have a lasting impact on fans.

This story matters because it highlights the emotional connection that fans have with their favorite characters. The Witcher series has a dedicated fan base, and the introduction of a new actor to play Geralt has sparked a lot of debate and discussion. Cockle's comments have helped to alleviate some of the concerns, and have reminded fans that the character is what truly matters, not the actor who plays him. The fact that Hemsworth has been able to win over Cockle, a veteran voice actor, is a testament to his talent and dedication to the role.

In conclusion, the story of Liam Hemsworth's portrayal of Geralt is a reminder that, in the world of voice-over and content creation, it's not just about the actor, but about the character and the world they inhabit. As voice-over professionals continue to push the boundaries of their craft, stories like this serve as a reminder of the power of their work to bring characters to life and connect with audiences on a deeper level. Whether you're a fan of The Witcher series or just a lover of great storytelling, this story is a must-watch, and a testament to the enduring power of compelling characters and talented voice actors.
