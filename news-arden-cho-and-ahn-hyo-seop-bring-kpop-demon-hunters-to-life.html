Arden Cho And Ahn Hyo Seop Bring KPop Demon Hunters To Life

Voice acting duo discusses roles on The Tonight Show

soompi
January 13, 2026
3 min read

Imagine being part of a world where KPop and fantasy collide, where the rhythms of Korean pop music meet the thrill of demon-slaying adventures. This is the realm of "KPop Demon Hunters," a project that has brought together talented voice actors Arden Cho and Ahn Hyo Seop. On January 12, they appeared on "The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon" to delve into their voice acting roles as Rumi and Jinu, sharing their passion for the project and the unique experience of breathing life into these characters.

According to soompi, their conversation on the show not only gave insight into their individual approaches to voice acting but also highlighted the distinctive blend of music and action that defines "KPop Demon Hunters." This blend is what sets the project apart, appealing to a wide range of audiences and showcasing the versatility of voice acting in contemporary media. The appearance of Cho and Ahn Hyo Seop on a major platform like "The Tonight Show" underscores the growing recognition of voice acting as a critical component of modern storytelling, from animation and video games to audiobooks and podcasts.

The voice-over industry, which has seen significant growth in recent years, is closely watching projects like "KPop Demon Hunters" for their innovative use of voice talent. Voice actors are no longer just behind-the-scenes figures; they are integral to the creation and success of a project, bringing depth and emotion to characters that might otherwise remain one-dimensional. For voice-over professionals, "KPop Demon Hunters" represents an exciting opportunity to explore new genres and styles, combining singing and acting talents in unique ways. This fusion challenges traditional notions of voice acting and opens up fresh avenues for creative expression and career development.

The impact of "KPop Demon Hunters" extends beyond the voice-over industry, touching on cultural and social spheres. It celebrates Korean pop culture, introducing it to new audiences and highlighting its global appeal. By merging KPop with fantasy elements, the project promotes cross-cultural understanding and appreciation, showing how different art forms can come together to create something entirely new and captivating. This blend of cultures and genres has the potential to inspire a new wave of creativity, encouraging collaborations that transcend traditional boundaries.

As Arden Cho and Ahn Hyo Seop's appearance on "The Tonight Show" demonstrates, the world of "KPop Demon Hunters" is not just about fantasy and music; it's about the power of voice to bring people together and to tell stories that resonate deeply. In an era where media consumption is more diverse than ever, projects like "KPop Demon Hunters" remind us of the enduring magic of storytelling and the critical role that voice actors play in conjuring worlds that inspire, entertain, and challenge us. As we look to the future of the voice-over industry, it's clear that innovations like "KPop Demon Hunters" will continue to push the boundaries of what is possible, offering voice talent unprecedented opportunities to shine and to bring their imagination and talent to the forefront of global entertainment.
