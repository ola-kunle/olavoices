Brian Cox to Voice God in Fox News Podcast

Scottish actor takes on divine role in 52-episode series

Vulture
November 20, 2025
3 min read

Imagine hearing the voice of God, guiding you through the life and teachings of Christ. It's a daunting task, yet Scottish actor Brian Cox has been chosen to take on this divine role in a 52-episode Fox News podcast. But, in a surprising twist, it seems Cox might not even be aware of his new gig.
