Anime Fans Outraged Over AI Voice Dubs, Dragon Ball Actor Speaks Out

Amazon's use of AI voice dubs sparks controversy in anime community

CinemaBlend
November 29, 2025
3 min read

Imagine your favorite childhood anime, now with a soulless, robotic voice. This is the harsh reality that anime fans are facing with the introduction of AI voice dubs. The news has sparked outrage among fans, and even voice actors are speaking out against the move. According to CinemaBlend, a Dragon Ball voice actor has blasted Amazon for their use of AI voice dubs, calling the product 'sh---y'.

The controversy began when fans noticed that some anime shows on Amazon were being dubbed with AI-generated voices. The move is seen as a cost-cutting measure, but it has been met with widespread criticism from fans and voice actors alike. The Dragon Ball voice actor's comments are a testament to the strong feelings that many in the industry have about the use of AI voice dubs.

The use of AI voice dubs is not just a matter of personal preference, but it also has significant implications for the voice-over industry. Voice actors are worried that the increasing use of AI voice dubs will lead to a loss of jobs and a devaluation of their craft. The voice-over industry is a multi-billion dollar market, with thousands of voice actors working on various projects, from anime and cartoons to video games and commercials. If AI voice dubs become the norm, it could have a devastating impact on the industry, leading to widespread job losses and a decline in the quality of voice-over work.

The impact of AI voice dubs on the voice-over industry cannot be overstated. Voice actors are not just concerned about their jobs, but also about the quality of the work that they do. AI voice dubs lack the nuance and emotion that a human voice actor can bring to a role, and this can have a significant impact on the overall quality of the production. Furthermore, the use of AI voice dubs raises important questions about the value of human creativity and the role of voice actors in the production process.

The controversy over AI voice dubs is not just about the technology itself, but also about the cultural and social implications of its use. Anime is a beloved genre that has brought people together from all over the world, and the use of AI voice dubs threatens to undermine the very thing that makes it so special. The Dragon Ball voice actor's comments are a call to action, urging fans and industry professionals to speak out against the use of AI voice dubs and to support human voice actors.

In conclusion, the use of AI voice dubs is a complex issue that has sparked controversy and debate in the anime community. While it may seem like a small matter to some, it has significant implications for the voice-over industry and the cultural and social impact of anime. As the Dragon Ball voice actor so eloquently put it, 'all they've done is make a sh---y product'. It is up to fans and industry professionals to speak out against the use of AI voice dubs and to support human voice actors, ensuring that the quality and integrity of anime productions are preserved for generations to come.
