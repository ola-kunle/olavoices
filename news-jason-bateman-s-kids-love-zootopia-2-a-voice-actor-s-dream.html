Family's sweet reactions to the Disney sequel

Imagine spending years working on a project, only to have your own children unimpressed by the final result. For voice actors like Bateman, this must be a dream come true - to see their own family enjoy the fruits of their labor.

The sequel to the beloved animated film has been making waves among moviegoers, and it's no surprise that Bateman's kids are among its fans. According to CinemaBlend, the new film has seemingly been making an impression on moviegoers, and it's a foregone conclusion that it will be a box office hit.
