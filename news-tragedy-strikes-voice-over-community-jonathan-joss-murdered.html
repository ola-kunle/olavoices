Tragedy Strikes Voice-Over Community: Jonathan Joss Murdered

Texas man indicted for murder of King of the Hill star

Breitbart News
November 19, 2025
3 min read

The voice-over community is reeling in shock and grief as news broke that Jonathan Joss, the talented voice actor behind many beloved characters, including his iconic role in King of the Hill, was tragically murdered. According to Breitbart News, a grand jury in Texas has indicted the man accused of killing Joss on a murder charge. This senseless act of violence has left friends, family, and fans of Joss stunned and heartbroken, struggling to come to terms with the loss of a brilliant talent and a kind soul.

Jonathan Joss was a highly respected and accomplished voice actor, with a career spanning multiple decades and numerous iconic roles. His work on King of the Hill, where he voiced the character of Juan Pedro, was particularly notable, earning him widespread recognition and acclaim. Joss's talent, dedication, and passion for his craft inspired countless voice-over professionals and aspiring actors, making his loss all the more devastating.
