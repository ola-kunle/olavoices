Tomb Raider Icon Says Goodbye, New Voice Rises

Camilla Luddington passes the torch to a new Lara Croft

Screen Rant
December 15, 2025
3 min read

Imagine stepping into the shoes of a legendary character, bringing them to life with your voice, and then saying goodbye. This is the bittersweet reality for Camilla Luddington, the voice behind Tomb Raider's Lara Croft, as she prepares to bid farewell to the role. According to Screen Rant, Luddington is passing the torch to a new voice actor, who will take on the iconic character in the series' next two entries.

The news comes ahead of a reboot, marking a significant change for the beloved video game franchise. For Luddington, it's the end of an era, one that she commemorated in a heartfelt post. As she looks back on her time as Lara Croft, fans and fellow voice actors alike can't help but feel a sense of nostalgia and appreciation for the work she's done. Luddington's dedication to the character has been evident throughout her tenure, and her departure will undoubtedly be felt.

For those unfamiliar with the Tomb Raider series, it's a cultural phenomenon that has captivated audiences for decades. The games follow the adventures of Lara Croft, a strong and determined heroine, as she navigates treacherous landscapes and battles formidable foes. The character has become an iconic symbol of female empowerment and a role model for many. As the series continues to evolve, it's essential to consider the impact of voice acting on its success. The right voice can make or break a character, and in the case of Lara Croft, Luddington's portrayal has been instrumental in shaping the character's identity.

The voice-over industry is intricately connected to the world of video games, with talented voice actors like Luddington bringing characters to life. As the industry continues to grow, voice actors face both opportunities and challenges. On one hand, the rise of video games and animated content has created a surge in demand for skilled voice talent. On the other hand, the increasing competition and evolving technology can make it difficult for voice actors to stand out and adapt. The departure of a prominent voice actor like Luddington serves as a reminder of the importance of versatility and resilience in the voice-over industry. It also highlights the need for voice actors to be open to new opportunities and willing to take on different roles, just like the new voice actor who will be taking on the iconic role of Lara Croft.

So, why does this matter? The impact of Luddington's departure extends beyond the world of video games. It's a reminder that, even in the digital realm, characters are brought to life by real people with real emotions and dedication. As we bid farewell to Luddington's Lara Croft, we're not just saying goodbye to a character; we're acknowledging the hard work and passion that went into creating her. This is a testament to the power of voice acting and the significant role it plays in shaping our emotional connections to the characters we love.

As we look to the future, it's exciting to think about the new voice that will bring Lara Croft to life. The opportunity for a fresh perspective and a new interpretation is a reminder that, even in the face of change, there is always room for growth and innovation. For voice actors, this serves as a powerful reminder of the importance of being open to new challenges and embracing the ever-evolving landscape of the voice-over industry. As we welcome the new voice of Lara Croft, we're not just welcoming a new character; we're embracing the next chapter in the story of a legendary heroine.
