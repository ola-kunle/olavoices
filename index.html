Transparent Pricing & Service Fees

My rates are designed to be competitive and transparent, ensuring you get high-quality voiceover services that fit your budget. Below are general guidelines; please contact me for a custom quote on your specific project.

Commercials & Promos

  • Local Web Use: Starting from $250
  • Regional Broadcast: Starting from $400
  • National Broadcast: Starting from $750+

(Rates include standard usage for 1 year. Extended usage or specific media will be quoted separately. Pricing varies by market size and media type.)

Narration & E-learning

  • Up to 5 minutes: Starting from $200
  • Per Finished Minute (PFM): $5 - $10 (depending on complexity and usage)
  • Audiobooks: Per Finished Hour (PFH) - please inquire for custom quote.

(Rates based on non-broadcast, internal, or e-learning usage. Commercial e-learning usage may incur additional fees. Final rate depends on script word count and complexity.)

IVR & On-Hold Messaging

  • Up to 10 prompts: Starting from $150
  • Each additional prompt: $10 - $15

(Flat rates for standard business use with perpetual licensing. Complex IVR systems with many prompts may require a custom quote.)

Important Considerations

  • Minimum Project Fee: All projects have a minimum fee of $150 to cover studio time and administrative costs.
  • Revisions: One round of minor script revisions (up to 10% of script, not a script rewrite) is included. Additional revisions or significant script changes after recording may incur extra fees.
  • Directed Sessions: Live directed sessions (via Source-Connect, Zoom, etc.) are available and may have an additional hourly fee (e.g., $50/hour after the first 30 minutes).
  • Rush Delivery: Projects requiring delivery within 24 hours may incur a rush fee (+50% of the total project cost), subject to availability.
  • Payment Terms: 50% upfront deposit required for new clients or projects over $500, with the balance due upon delivery. Payment accepted via Stripe, PayPal, or Bank Transfer.
