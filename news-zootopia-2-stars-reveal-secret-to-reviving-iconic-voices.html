Zootopia 2 Stars Reveal Secret to Reviving Iconic Voices

Jason Bateman and Ginnifer Goodwin share their journey

ComicBook.com
November 21, 2025
3 min read

Imagine stepping back into a role that defined your career, a decade after the fact. For Jason Bateman and Ginnifer Goodwin, the stars of Disney's beloved animated film Zootopia, this was the daunting task at hand. As they prepared to reprise their roles as Nick Wilde and Judy Hopps in the highly anticipated sequel, Zootopia 2, they had to dig deep to rediscover the spark that made their characters so endearing to audiences worldwide. According to ComicBook.com, the duo recently opened up about their experience getting back into character, and the process was nothing short of remarkable.

Zootopia, released in 2016, was a groundbreaking film that earned widespread critical acclaim and grossed over a billion dollars at the box office. The movie's clever storytelling, gorgeous animation, and thoughtful exploration of complex social issues resonated with viewers of all ages. As the voice actors behind the film's two main characters, Bateman and Goodwin played a pivotal role in bringing the story to life. Their performances were widely praised, and their characters have since become ingrained in popular culture.
