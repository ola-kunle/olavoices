KPop Demon Hunters Sweeps Annie Awards

10 Nominations Including Best Voice Acting

GamesRadar+
January 5, 2026
3 min read

Imagine a world where music, animation, and storytelling collide in a way that leaves you breathless. For fans of KPop Demon Hunters, that world is now a reality. According to GamesRadar+, this animated sensation has just received a whopping 10 Annie Award nominations, including Best Feature, Best Music, and Best Voice Acting. The film's impact is undeniable, and its nomination tally is matched only by Pixar's Elio in 2026.

The Annie Awards are a benchmark of excellence in the animation industry, and KPop Demon Hunters' numerous nominations are a testament to its captivating story, stunning animation, and memorable voice acting. For those who may be unfamiliar, the Annie Awards honor outstanding achievements in animation, and a nomination is a significant recognition of a film's quality and impact.
