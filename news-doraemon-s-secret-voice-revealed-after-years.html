Doraemon's Secret Voice Revealed After Years

Sonal Kaushal's journey as a voice actress

The Times of India
January 8, 2026
3 min read

Imagine being the voice behind your favorite childhood characters, yet keeping it a secret from the world. This is the story of Sonal Kaushal, the talented voice actress behind Doraemon and Shinchan. According to The Times of India, Kaushal began her career at the tender age of 8, but her talent remained hidden from her peers until her father accidentally revealed it at school. This anecdote not only showcases Kaushal's humility but also highlights the sacrifices voice actors make to bring joy to others.

As a voice actress, Kaushal's journey is a testament to the dedication and passion required in the voice-over industry. With a career spanning decades, she has become a household name in India, synonymous with the beloved characters she has voiced. However, her story also underscores the challenges faced by voice actors, who often work behind the scenes, their voices recognized but their faces unknown. The voice-over industry, which has grown exponentially with the rise of animation and digital content, relies on talented individuals like Kaushal who bring characters to life.

The voice-over industry is a significant sector in the global entertainment market, with opportunities for voice actors to work on a wide range of projects, from animated series and films to video games and advertisements. According to industry reports, the global voice-over market is expected to continue growing, driven by increasing demand for digital content. For voice-over professionals, Kaushal's story serves as a reminder of the importance of perseverance and the need to continuously develop their craft. It also highlights the opportunities available for voice actors to work on diverse projects, from dubbing international shows to creating content for local audiences.

Sonal Kaushal's story matters because it touches on the emotional and social impact of voice acting. Her characters have brought joy to countless children and adults alike, transcending linguistic and cultural barriers. In a world where content creation is becoming increasingly democratized, the role of voice actors like Kaushal cannot be overstated. They are the unsung heroes of the entertainment industry, using their talents to transport audiences to new worlds and evoke emotions that linger long after the screen fades to black.

As we reflect on Kaushal's journey, we are reminded of the power of voice acting to inspire, to educate, and to entertain. In an industry where technology is constantly evolving, the human voice remains a constant, capable of conveying emotions and connecting with audiences in a way that transcends borders and cultures. As the voice-over industry continues to grow, it is essential to recognize the contributions of talented individuals like Sonal Kaushal, who have dedicated their lives to bringing characters to life and touching the hearts of millions.
