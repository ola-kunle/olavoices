Walton Goggins Revives Ghoul Role in Fallout 76 Expansion

Renowned actor's reprisal delights developers and fans alike

PC Gamer
December 18, 2025
3 min read

Imagine stepping into a post-apocalyptic world, where the lines between good and evil are blurred, and the only constant is the pursuit of survival. This is the world of Fallout 76, and it just got a whole lot more interesting with the return of a beloved character, the Ghoul, voiced by none other than Walton Goggins. According to PC Gamer, Goggins reprised his role as the Ghoul in the game's latest expansion, set in the wild Wasteland of Ohio, and it was a huge hit among the developers.

For fans of the Fallout series, this news is a dream come true. The Ghoul, a complex and intriguing character, has been a part of the franchise for years, and Goggins' portrayal has been widely praised. But what makes this story even more compelling is the impact it has on the voice-over industry. As a voice actor, Goggins' ability to bring depth and nuance to his characters is a testament to the power of voice acting in gaming. His reprisal of the Ghoul role is not only a nod to the fans but also a demonstration of the importance of voice talent in creating immersive gaming experiences.

The voice-over industry is a vital part of the gaming world, and this news highlights the significance of voice actors in bringing characters to life. As the gaming industry continues to evolve, the demand for talented voice actors who can convey emotion, complexity, and depth is on the rise. For voice-over professionals, this creates new opportunities for collaboration and creativity. It also underscores the importance of developing a wide range of skills, from acting to improvisation, to stay competitive in the industry.
