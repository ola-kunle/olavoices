Airi Taira Joins Doraemon Film Cast

20th Century Boys actress to voice soldier in new film

Anime News Network
January 12, 2026
3 min read

Imagine being part of a beloved franchise that has captivated audiences for decades, bringing joy and excitement to fans of all ages. For Airi Taira, this dream has become a reality as she joins the cast of the 2026 Doraemon film, Eiga Doraemon: Shin Nobita no Kaitei Kiganj (Doraemon the Movie: New Nobita and the Castle of the Undersea Devil). According to Anime News Network, the staff for the film announced on Monday that Taira, known for her role in 20th Century Boys, will voice a soldier in the upcoming movie, set to release on February 27.

The Doraemon franchise has been a staple of Japanese pop culture since its creation in 1969, with numerous films, TV shows, and other media showcasing the adventures of the titular character and his friends. The latest film promises to deliver the same blend of action, humor, and heart that fans have come to expect from the series. With Taira's addition to the cast, the movie is sure to attract even more attention from fans and newcomers alike.

For those unfamiliar with Airi Taira, she is a talented actress who has made a name for herself in the Japanese entertainment industry. Her role in 20th Century Boys, a live-action adaptation of the popular manga series, showcased her versatility and range as a performer. Her involvement in the Doraemon film is a testament to her growing reputation and the demand for her talents.
