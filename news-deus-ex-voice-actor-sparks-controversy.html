Deus Ex Voice Actor Sparks Controversy

Elias Toufexis reveals no new game in 2026

Eurogamer.net
January 2, 2026
3 min read

Imagine being a die-hard fan of the Deus Ex series, eagerly awaiting the next installment, only to have your hopes crushed by the voice behind the beloved main character, Adam Jensen. According to Eurogamer.net, Elias Toufexis, the voice actor behind robotic employee of the year and Deus Ex main character Adam Jensen, has assured fans that a new entry in the sci-fi series will not be coming in 2026, jokingly attributing the delay to the people in charge being 'psychopaths'. This revelation has sent shockwaves through the gaming community, leaving fans wondering what the future holds for the franchise.

For those unfamiliar with the series, Deus Ex is a critically acclaimed sci-fi role-playing game that explores complex themes such as conspiracy, artificial intelligence, and human augmentation. The series has garnered a loyal following over the years, and the absence of a new game in 2026 is sure to disappoint many.
