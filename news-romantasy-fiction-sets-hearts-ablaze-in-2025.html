Romantasy Fiction Sets Hearts Ablaze in 2025

A genre that combines romance and fantasy

Independent.ie
December 26, 2025
3 min read

Imagine being transported to a world of magic and wonder, where faeries and warriors reign supreme, and the boundaries of love and desire are pushed to the limit. This is the world of romantasy, a genre that has captured the hearts of many, including writer Chrissie Russell, who confesses to being 'all hot and bothered' by its glorious escapism. According to Independent.ie, Russell's obsession with romantasy has become an all-consuming passion, one that she indulges in even in the most mundane of settings, like a car park outside Tesco.

As she sits in her car, ready to do the big shop, Russell finds herself drawn into a world of 'shadow-wielding dragon-rides' and 'gravel-voiced males' who introduce her to 'all seven gods'. This is a world that is both familiar and yet, utterly fantastical, where the rules of reality are bent and twisted to create a unique and captivating narrative. The genre of romantasy has been gaining popularity in recent years, with its unique blend of romance, fantasy, and adventure proving to be a winning combination for many readers.

But what makes romantasy so appealing to its fans? Is it the promise of escape from the stresses of everyday life, or the thrill of exploring new and uncharted worlds? According to Russell, it's a combination of both, as well as the opportunity to indulge in 'lots of sex' and 'intriguing character development'. Whatever the reason, it's clear that romantasy has become a major player in the world of fiction, with its own dedicated fan base and a growing list of authors and creators who are pushing the boundaries of the genre.

So, what does this mean for the voice-over industry? As the demand for romantasy content continues to grow, voice actors and content creators are presented with a unique opportunity to bring these fantastical worlds to life. Whether it's through audiobooks, animations, or video games, the voice-over industry plays a critical role in shaping the narrative and atmosphere of romantasy stories. Voice actors who can convey the emotion, passion, and intensity of these stories will be in high demand, as creators seek to immerse their audiences in the world of romantasy. This presents both opportunities and challenges for voice talent, as they must be able to convey the complexity and depth of these characters and worlds, while also navigating the nuances of the genre.

As the popularity of romantasy continues to grow, it's likely that we'll see more and more voice actors and content creators getting involved in the genre. This could lead to new and exciting opportunities for voice talent, as well as the creation of new and innovative content that pushes the boundaries of the genre. But it also raises important questions about the impact of romantasy on our culture and society. Does the genre's emphasis on escapism and fantasy have a positive or negative impact on our mental health and wellbeing? And what does it say about our desires and values as a society?

Ultimately, the rise of romantasy is a reminder of the power of storytelling to capture our imaginations and transport us to new and exciting worlds. As we look to the future, it will be interesting to see how the voice-over industry continues to shape and influence the genre, and what new and innovative content emerges as a result. One thing is certain, however: the world of romantasy is here to stay, and it's going to be a wild and thrilling ride.
