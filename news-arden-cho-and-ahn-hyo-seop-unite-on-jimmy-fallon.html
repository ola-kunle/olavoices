Arden Cho And Ahn Hyo Seop Unite On Jimmy Fallon

Discussing 'KPop Demon Hunters' Voice Acting Roles

soompi
January 13, 2026
3 min read

Imagine being part of a project that combines the thrill of K-pop with the excitement of demon-hunting adventures. For Arden Cho and Ahn Hyo Seop, this fantasy has become a reality as they voice the characters Rumi and Jinu in 'KPop Demon Hunters'. On January 12, the duo appeared on 'The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon' to share their experiences and insights into the making of this unique animated series.

According to soompi, their conversation on the show delved into their roles, the 'free' covers, and more, giving fans a glimpse into the creative process behind 'KPop Demon Hunters'. This project is not just about entertainment; it represents a growing trend in the voice-over industry where actors are pushing the boundaries of storytelling through animation and voice acting.

The voice-over industry has witnessed significant growth in recent years, with the rise of animation, video games, and audiobooks. This growth has created new opportunities for voice actors to explore different genres and characters, from dramatic roles to comedic ones. The success of 'KPop Demon Hunters' and similar projects can be attributed to the talented voice actors who bring these characters to life. Arden Cho and Ahn Hyo Seop's appearance on 'The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon' highlights the importance of voice acting in the entertainment industry and the impact it has on the audience.

The connection between 'KPop Demon Hunters' and the voice-over industry is more than just about the project itself; it's about the opportunities and challenges it creates for voice talent. With the increasing demand for voice actors in animation and video games, projects like 'KPop Demon Hunters' provide a platform for voice actors to showcase their skills and versatility. Moreover, the blending of K-pop and demon-hunting themes in 'KPop Demon Hunters' opens up new avenues for content creation, catering to a diverse audience with unique interests.

The cultural and social impact of 'KPop Demon Hunters' cannot be overstated. The series has the potential to bridge the gap between different cultures, introducing K-pop to a broader audience and promoting cross-cultural understanding. The fact that Arden Cho and Ahn Hyo Seop, two talented actors from different backgrounds, are part of this project, emphasizes the power of collaboration and diversity in the entertainment industry.

In conclusion, Arden Cho and Ahn Hyo Seop's discussion on 'The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon' about 'KPop Demon Hunters' is more than just a promotional appearance; it's a testament to the evolving voice-over industry and its capacity to push boundaries and challenge norms. As the industry continues to grow, it will be exciting to see how voice actors, like Cho and Seop, contribute to its development and shape the future of storytelling.
