Understanding Pricing: What to Expect When You Hire a Nigerian Voice Actor

Pricing for professional voice-over services, especially for e-learning, typically depends on several factors:

  • Word Count / Finished Minutes: This is often the primary driver. E-learning is frequently priced per word or per finished minute, as it requires precise timing and often includes extensive post-production.
  • Usage & Licensing: Where and for how long the audio will be used (e.g., internal corporate training vs. publicly available online course) impacts the licensing fee.
  • Complexity of Content: Highly technical or specialized content may require more time for research and accurate pronunciation.
  • Turnaround Time: Rush jobs requiring immediate delivery may incur an additional fee.
  • Editing & Post-Production: Basic editing (noise reduction, breaths removal) is usually included. More extensive requests like splitting files, naming, or syncing to video might be additional.

While specific rates are always project-dependent, you can expect professional Nigerian voice actors with international experience to charge anywhere from $0.20 to $0.50 per word or $150 to $500 per finished minute for standard e-learning projects. Larger projects or those with extensive usage rights may be quoted at a custom project rate. My goal is always to provide fair, transparent pricing that reflects the value and quality you receive.
