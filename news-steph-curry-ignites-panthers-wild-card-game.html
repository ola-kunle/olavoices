Steph Curry Ignites Panthers' Wild Card Game

NBA star narrates pregame hype video with passion

Yahoo Entertainment
January 10, 2026
3 min read

Imagine the thrill of a Wild Card playoff game, the crowd's energy electric, and the players ready to give it their all. According to Yahoo Entertainment, the Panthers recently added an extra layer of excitement to their pregame routine by featuring none other than NBA superstar Steph Curry in their hype video. As the narrator, Curry brought his signature swagger and charisma to the video, no doubt inspiring the team and getting fans pumped for the big game against the Rams.
