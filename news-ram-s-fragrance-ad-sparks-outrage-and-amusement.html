Ram's Fragrance Ad Sparks Outrage and Amusement

Jalopnik calls out Ram's questionable marketing tactics

Jalopnik
December 2, 2025
3 min read

Imagine watching a commercial that's supposed to be about a new fragrance, but it ends up being a laughable display of attempted masculinity. That's what's happening with Ram's latest ad, which has left many scratching their heads. According to Jalopnik, the commercial starts with a man's hand reaching for the fragrance bottle from the passenger seat of a truck, already raising questions about the intended message.

The ad's slogan, 'This won't take long,' is voiced by a female voice-over actor, who seems to be in on the joke. It's starting to feel like Ram is specifically trolling us, as Jalopnik puts it. The publication has been following Ram's marketing efforts, and this latest move has them questioning the company's strategy.

For those who may not be familiar with Ram, the company is known for its trucks and has been trying to rebrand itself as a lifestyle company. This fragrance ad is part of that effort, but it's clear that the execution has fallen flat. The ad's attempt to appeal to a certain type of masculinity has been met with eye-rolls and laughter.

But what does this mean for the voice-over industry? Voice actors are often the unseen forces behind commercials, bringing scripts to life with their talents. In this case, the female voice-over actor's deadpan delivery of the slogan adds to the ad's comedic effect. This highlights the importance of voice-over work in shaping the tone and message of a commercial. Voice actors have the power to make or break an ad, and their talents should not be underestimated.

The voice-over industry is a significant sector, with many professionals working behind the scenes to bring content to life. This ad is a reminder that voice-over work is not just about reading from a script; it's about bringing a character to life and conveying a message. For voice-over professionals, this ad presents an opportunity to showcase their range and versatility. However, it also highlights the challenges of working with questionable scripts and direction.

So, why does this matter? It matters because it reflects a broader cultural conversation about masculinity and marketing. Companies are trying to appeal to certain demographics, but they often end up missing the mark. This ad is a prime example of how not to do it. The backlash against the ad is not just about the commercial itself; it's about the values and attitudes that it represents.

In conclusion, Ram's fragrance ad may have been intended to be a bold statement, but it's ended up being a laughingstock. As we watch the ad and read the reactions to it, we're reminded of the power of voice-over work in shaping our perceptions. Voice actors are not just talented individuals; they're also cultural commentators, bringing their unique perspectives to the content they create. As we move forward, it's essential to recognize the value of voice-over work and the impact it has on our cultural landscape.
