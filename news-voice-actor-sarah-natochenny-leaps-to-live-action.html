Voice Actor Sarah Natochenny Leaps to Live Action

From Pokémon to Raccoon, a 19-year journey

IndieWire
November 30, 2025
3 min read

Imagine spending nearly two decades bringing a beloved character to life, only to face the daunting task of transitioning to a new medium. For Sarah Natochenny, the voice of Ash Ketchum in the English dub of Pokémon, this was the reality she faced. According to IndieWire, Natochenny has finally made the leap to live action, joining the set of Tim Heidecker's Raccoon. This milestone marks a significant turning point in her career, one that will undoubtedly inspire countless voice actors around the world.

Natochenny's journey began 19 years ago, when she first landed the role of Ash Ketchum. Since then, she has become an iconic figure in the world of Pokémon, beloved by fans of all ages. However, as she delved deeper into the industry, she encountered numerous obstacles that threatened to derail her career. Despite these challenges, Natochenny persevered, leveraging social media and convention appearances to shape her career and build a loyal fan base. Her determination and resilience have paid off, as she now stands on the cusp of a new chapter in her acting career.
