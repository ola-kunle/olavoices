Idris Elba's Ad-Lib Steals the Scene in Zootopia 2

Ke Huy Quan pitches Gary's last scene in Disney's sequel

Bleeding Cool News
November 30, 2025
3 min read

Imagine being part of a project where your voice can bring a character to life, but the animation process is so lengthy that it leaves little room for spontaneity. This is the reality for voice actors in animated films, where every line, every inflection, and every emotion must be carefully crafted and planned. Yet, in a recent revelation, Zootopia 2 co-director Jared Bush shared that one of Chief Bogo's lines was an ad-lib from the talented Idris Elba, adding a layer of authenticity to the character.

According to Bleeding Cool News, this ad-lib was a testament to Elba's exceptional voice acting skills, which have captivated audiences worldwide. The news also highlighted Ke Huy Quan's contribution to the film, as he pitched the idea for Gary's last scene, showcasing the collaborative efforts that go into creating an animated masterpiece. This level of creative input from voice actors is a rarity in the industry, where the animation process often takes precedence over voice acting.

The world of animation is no stranger to talented voice actors, with many bringing their unique skills to the table. From the comedic genius of Patton Oswalt to the dramatic flair of Benedict Cumberbatch, voice actors have the power to elevate a character and make them relatable to audiences. However, the lengthy animation process can sometimes restrict their ability to improvise or try new things, making Elba's ad-lib all the more remarkable.

The voice-over industry is a significant aspect of content creation, with voice actors playing a crucial role in bringing characters to life. The news about Elba's ad-lib and Quan's pitch highlights the importance of voice actors' creative input in the animation process. It also underscores the opportunities and challenges that exist for voice talent in the industry. With the rise of animated films and series, voice actors have more opportunities than ever to showcase their skills and bring unique characters to life. However, they must also navigate the challenges of working within a restrictive animation process, where every line and every movement is carefully planned and executed.

This story matters because it showcases the impact that voice actors can have on a film's success. It also highlights the emotional connection that audiences form with characters, which is often facilitated by the talented voice actors behind them. As the animation industry continues to evolve, it will be interesting to see how voice actors' roles change and how they adapt to new technologies and processes. One thing is certain, though - the voice-over industry will remain a vital part of content creation, bringing characters to life and captivating audiences worldwide.

In conclusion, Idris Elba's ad-lib in Zootopia 2 is a testament to the power of voice acting and the impact it can have on a film's success. As the voice-over industry continues to grow and evolve, it will be exciting to see how voice actors push the boundaries of their craft and bring new characters to life. Whether you're a seasoned voice actor or just starting out, this story is a reminder of the importance of creativity, spontaneity, and collaboration in the animation process.
