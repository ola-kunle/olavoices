Detective Conan Revival Brings New Voices to the Scene

Fresh cast introduces the classic anime to a new audience

Anime News Network
January 9, 2026
4 min read

Imagine being a part of a legacy that has been thrilling audiences for decades. For voice actors Molly Zhang, Mauricio Ortiz-Segura, and ADR Director Ayu, that reality is now. As the iconic Detective Conan anime series approaches its 30th anniversary, a new batch of dubbed episodes is set to introduce the beloved franchise to a fresh generation of fans. According to Anime News Network, this milestone marks a significant moment in the series' history, bringing the original anime to a new audience.

The Detective Conan franchise, which has been a staple of Japanese pop culture since its debut in 1996, has grown to become one of the most popular and enduring anime series of all time. With its unique blend of mystery, adventure, and humor, it has captivated audiences worldwide, inspiring numerous adaptations, including movies, TV specials, and even a live-action drama. As the series continues to evolve, its impact on the voice-over industry cannot be overstated. The introduction of a new cast, including Molly Zhang as Conan and Mauricio Ortiz-Segura as Shinichi Kudo, highlights the ever-changing landscape of voice acting and the importance of adapting to new audiences and markets.

The voice-over industry is a vital component of the anime world, as it brings characters to life and helps to convey the emotional depth and complexity of a story. For voice actors, the opportunity to work on a project like Detective Conan is a dream come true. It not only allows them to showcase their talents but also to be part of a legacy that has been entertaining fans for generations. The new dub batch releases also create opportunities for voice-over professionals to explore new roles and challenges, pushing the boundaries of their craft and contributing to the growth of the industry as a whole.

As the voice-over industry continues to evolve, it is essential to recognize the significance of projects like Detective Conan. As fans around the world prepare to embark on a new adventure with Conan and his friends, the voice-over industry is poised to play a vital role in shaping the future of anime and beyond.
