Fallout TV Show Leaves Fans Heartbroken

Felicia Day's character absence sparks disappointment

PC Gamer
December 7, 2025
3 min read

Imagine stepping into a post-apocalyptic world, only to find that a beloved character is nowhere to be found. For fans of the Fallout series, this is the harsh reality they're facing with the announcement that Felicia Day's character from Fallout: New Vegas will not be appearing in the second season of the Fallout TV show. According to PC Gamer, Day expressed her gratitude for the role, stating it 'is one of the best roles I've ever got to play,' making her absence even more poignant.

The Fallout series has a dedicated fan base, and the TV show's visit to the Mojave in its second season had fans eagerly anticipating the return of familiar faces and locations. The Kings, the NCR, and other beloved elements from Fallout: New Vegas were expected to make an appearance, but Day's character will not be among them. This news has left fans feeling disappointed and heartbroken, as they had been looking forward to seeing their favorite characters brought to life on screen.

For those who may not be familiar with the Fallout series, it's a beloved franchise that has been entertaining gamers for decades. The series is known for its rich storytelling, engaging characters, and immersive worlds, making it a favorite among gamers and science fiction fans alike. Fallout: New Vegas, in particular, has a special place in the hearts of many fans, and Felicia Day's character is a big part of that. Her absence from the TV show is a significant blow to fans who were hoping to see her character's story continue.
