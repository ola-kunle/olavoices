## Lead Scraping Strategy

### FREE Tools:

**1. LinkedIn Sales Navigator (Free Trial - 1 month)**
- Search for decision makers
- Export to CSV
- Use filters: Company size, Industry, Location

**2. Hunter.io (FREE tier: 50 email searches/month)**
- Find email addresses from LinkedIn profiles
- Verify emails before sending
- https://hunter.io

**3. Apollo.io (FREE tier: 50 credits/month)**
- B2B contact database
- Email finder + verification
- https://apollo.io

**4. Google Sheets + Manual Research**
- Search LinkedIn manually
- Company websites for contact info
- Crunchbase for gaming startups

### Manual Research Process:

**For NGOs:**
1. Google: "NGO Nigeria health communications"
2. Check their website → Team page → Find Communications/Media lead
3. LinkedIn search: "[NGO name] communications director"
4. Use Hunter.io to find email
5. Add to spreadsheet

**For Gaming:**
1. Search: "indie game studios African characters"
2. Check game credits on MobyGames/IGDB
3. Find Audio Director on LinkedIn
4. Check studio website for contact
5. Add to spreadsheet
