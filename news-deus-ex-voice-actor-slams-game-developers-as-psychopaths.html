Deus Ex Voice Actor Slams Game Developers as Psychopaths

Elias Toufexis speaks out on lack of new Deus Ex game

Wccftech
January 2, 2026
3 min read

Imagine being the voice behind a beloved character, only to see the franchise you've dedicated yourself to being left in limbo. This is the harsh reality for Elias Toufexis, the voice actor behind Adam Jensen in the Deus Ex series. According to Wccftech, Toufexis has expressed his frustration and disappointment with the people in charge, calling them 'psychopaths' for not producing a new Deus Ex game in 2026.

The Deus Ex series has been a staple in the gaming world, known for its engaging storylines and immersive gameplay. As the voice of Adam Jensen, Toufexis has been an integral part of the franchise's success. However, with no new game on the horizon, fans are left wondering what's next for the series. Toufexis's comments have sparked a heated debate, with many fans taking to social media to express their support for the voice actor and their desire for a new Deus Ex game.

The lack of a new Deus Ex game has significant implications for the voice-over industry. As a voice actor, Toufexis's livelihood is dependent on the production of new games and content. When a franchise is put on hold, it not only affects the voice actors but also the entire production team, from writers to sound designers. This can lead to a ripple effect, impacting the livelihoods of many professionals in the industry. Furthermore, the absence of new content can also lead to a stagnation of creativity and innovation, as voice actors and other professionals are not given the opportunity to push the boundaries of their craft.

The voice-over industry is built on the creation of new and exciting content. When a franchise like Deus Ex is not producing new games, it creates a void in the market that can be difficult to fill. Voice actors like Toufexis rely on the production of new games to showcase their talents and to stay relevant in the industry. The lack of new content can also lead to a brain drain, as talented voice actors and other professionals may seek opportunities in other industries. This can have a lasting impact on the voice-over industry, as the loss of talent and creativity can be difficult to recover from.

The emotional and social impact of Toufexis's comments cannot be overstated. As a voice actor, he has formed a deep connection with the fans of the Deus Ex series, and his frustration is palpable. The comments have sparked a sense of solidarity among fans, with many taking to social media to express their support for Toufexis and their desire for a new Deus Ex game. This sense of community and camaraderie is a testament to the power of the voice-over industry, where actors and fans can come together to celebrate their shared passion for gaming and storytelling.

In conclusion, the comments made by Elias Toufexis are a stark reminder of the challenges faced by voice actors and other professionals in the industry. The lack of a new Deus Ex game is not just a disappointment for fans, but also a harsh reality for those who have dedicated their careers to the franchise. As the voice-over industry continues to evolve, it's essential that we recognize the importance of creating new and exciting content, not just for the sake of the fans, but also for the talented professionals who bring these stories to life.
