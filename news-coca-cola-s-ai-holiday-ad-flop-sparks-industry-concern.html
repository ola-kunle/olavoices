Coca-Cola's AI Holiday Ad Flop Sparks Industry Concern

70,000 prompts yield underwhelming results, raising questions

Boredpanda.com
November 19, 2025
3 min read

Imagine a holiday season without the warmth and magic of a well-crafted advertisement to get you in the mood. For many, Coca-Cola's holiday commercials are a staple of the season, but this year's AI-generated ad has left a lot to be desired. According to Boredpanda.com, the multi-billion dollar beverage company took a staggering 70,000 prompts to create a lackluster holiday commercial that has been met with widespread criticism.

The news has sparked a heated debate about the role of AI in content creation, with many questioning the value of relying on artificial intelligence to produce emotionally resonant advertisements. Coca-Cola's foray into AI-generated content is not the company's first, but it is certainly one of its most notable missteps. Last year, the company faced backlash for its AI-generated holiday commercial, and it seems that this year's attempt has not fared much better.

As the advertising industry continues to evolve, the use of AI in content creation is becoming increasingly prevalent. However, the failure of Coca-Cola's AI-generated holiday commercial raises important questions about the limitations of artificial intelligence in creating emotionally engaging content. For voice-over professionals, this story is particularly relevant, as it highlights the importance of human touch and creativity in content creation. The voice-over industry is built on the ability of talented voice actors to bring scripts to life, conveying emotion and nuance in a way that AI systems currently cannot. The fact that Coca-Cola's AI-generated commercial required 70,000 prompts to produce a lackluster result underscores the value of human voice-over talent in creating compelling and engaging content.

The implications of this story extend far beyond the advertising industry, speaking to the very heart of what makes us human. In an era where technology is increasingly pervasive, it is easy to lose sight of the importance of human connection and creativity. The failure of Coca-Cola's AI-generated holiday commercial serves as a reminder that, despite the advances of artificial intelligence, there is no substitute for the warmth and emotion that a human voice can bring to a story. As we move forward in this increasingly tech-driven world, it is essential that we do not lose sight of the value of human creativity and connection.

In conclusion, the failure of Coca-Cola's AI-generated holiday commercial is a wake-up call for the advertising industry, highlighting the importance of human touch and creativity in content creation. For voice-over professionals, this story serves as a reminder of the value and relevance of their craft, and the need to continue pushing the boundaries of what is possible with human voice and emotion. As we look to the future, one thing is clear: the human voice will always be the most powerful tool in storytelling, and it is up to us to ensure that it remains at the heart of content creation.
