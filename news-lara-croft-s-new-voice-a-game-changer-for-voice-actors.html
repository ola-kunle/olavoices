Lara Croft's New Voice: A Game-Changer for Voice Actors

Alix Wilton Regan brings Tomb Raider to life

PC Gamer
December 12, 2025
3 min read

Imagine being the voice behind one of the most iconic characters in gaming history. For Alix Wilton Regan, that dream has become a reality. According to PC Gamer, Regan has been cast as the new voice of Lara Croft in the upcoming Tomb Raider games, including Legacy of Atlantis and Tomb Raider: Cat. This news is not only exciting for fans of the franchise but also marks a significant milestone for the voice-over industry.

Regan's impressive resume boasts credits in notable games such as Cyberpunk 2077, Lies of P, Dragon Age, and Assassin's Creed. Her experience and talent have undoubtedly prepared her for this iconic role. The Game Awards recently gave fans a sneak peek at the new face of Tomb Raider, and the anticipation is building. As the gaming industry continues to evolve, the importance of voice acting cannot be overstated. A good voice actor can bring a character to life, making them relatable and memorable.

The voice-over industry is a vital component of content creation, and this news highlights the opportunities available to voice actors. With the rise of gaming and animation, the demand for talented voice actors has increased. This shift creates new challenges and opportunities for voice-over professionals, as they must adapt to the changing landscape and continually improve their craft. For instance, voice actors must now consider factors such as motion capture and performance capture, which require a deeper understanding of the character's physicality and emotions.

The impact of Regan's casting extends beyond the gaming community. It also showcases the importance of representation and diversity in the industry. As a veteran voice actor, Regan's success demonstrates that experience and talent can lead to significant roles. This news should inspire aspiring voice actors to pursue their passions and strive for excellence in their craft. Moreover, it highlights the need for greater diversity in voice casting, ensuring that characters are represented in a way that is authentic and relatable to their respective communities.

The cultural significance of Lara Croft cannot be ignored. As a strong and independent female character, she has become an icon for many gamers. Regan's voice will undoubtedly bring a new layer of depth to the character, making her more relatable and human. This is a testament to the power of voice acting, which can elevate a character from a mere concept to a living, breathing person. As the gaming industry continues to push boundaries and explore new frontiers, the role of voice actors will only continue to grow in importance.

In conclusion, Alix Wilton Regan's casting as the new voice of Lara Croft is a significant moment for the voice-over industry. It highlights the opportunities available to talented voice actors and demonstrates the importance of representation and diversity in the industry. As we look to the future of gaming and content creation, it is clear that voice actors will play an increasingly vital role in bringing characters to life. The question is, what's next for this talented industry, and how will voice actors continue to shape the world of gaming and beyond?
