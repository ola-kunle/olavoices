Voice Actor Gil Gerard Passes Away at 82

Beloved actor's legacy lives on in voice-over community

Anime News Network
December 18, 2025
3 min read

The world of entertainment has lost a beloved figure with the passing of Gil Gerard, a veteran actor and voice actor, at the age of 82. According to Anime News Network, Gerard succumbed to a rare and viciously aggressive form of cancer on Tuesday, leaving behind a legacy that will be deeply felt in the voice-over community. Gerard's extensive career spanned multiple decades, with notable roles in TV shows such as 'Buck Rogers' and voice acting credits in 'Transformers: Robots in Disguise'.

Gerard's journey in the entertainment industry began in the 1960s, with early appearances in film and television. His breakout role came in 1979 when he played the titular character in the TV series 'Buck Rogers in the 25th Century'. This iconic role cemented his status as a science fiction icon and paved the way for future generations of actors. Throughout his career, Gerard demonstrated his versatility by taking on a wide range of roles, from drama to comedy, and eventually, voice acting.

The voice-over industry, in particular, has lost a talented contributor with Gerard's passing. As a voice actor, he brought characters to life in animated series and films, showcasing his ability to adapt to different roles and genres. According to Anime News Network, Gerard's voice acting credits include 'Transformers: Robots in Disguise', a role that highlights his capacity to connect with audiences of all ages. The voice-over community will deeply feel his absence, as his contributions have inspired many aspiring voice actors and will continue to do so through his body of work.

Gerard's passing serves as a reminder of the significant impact that voice actors have on our lives. They have the power to evoke emotions, spark imagination, and create lasting connections with their audiences. The voice-over industry is a vital part of the entertainment landscape, providing opportunities for talented individuals to bring stories to life and touch hearts. As the industry continues to evolve, it is essential to recognize the value and dedication of voice actors like Gerard, who have dedicated their careers to perfecting their craft.

The news of Gerard's passing has sent shockwaves through the entertainment community, with many fans and colleagues taking to social media to pay tribute to his legacy. His contributions to the world of science fiction and voice acting will be deeply missed, but his memory will live on through the countless characters he brought to life. As we reflect on Gerard's remarkable career, we are reminded of the profound impact that voice actors can have on our lives and the importance of preserving their legacy for future generations.

In conclusion, the passing of Gil Gerard is a poignant reminder of the significant role that voice actors play in shaping our entertainment landscape. As we mourn the loss of this talented individual, we also celebrate his remarkable contributions to the voice-over community and the lasting impact he will have on the industry. His legacy will continue to inspire voice actors and fans alike, ensuring that his memory lives on for years to come.
