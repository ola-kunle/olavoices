Voice Actor's $800 Payment for Billion-Dollar Game Sparks Outrage

NBA Jam's 'Boomshakalaka' voice actor reveals shocking underpayment

Notebookcheck.net
December 1, 2025
3 min read

Imagine pouring your heart and soul into a project, only to be paid a mere fraction of its worth. This is the shocking reality for Tim Kitzrow, the veteran voice actor behind the iconic 'Boomshakalaka' catchphrase in the 1993 arcade hit NBA Jam. According to Notebookcheck.net, Kitzrow was paid a staggering $800 for his work, despite the game generating an estimated $1-2 billion in revenue. The sheer disparity between Kitzrow's payment and the game's earnings is a harsh reminder of the often-exploitative nature of the entertainment industry.

The story of NBA Jam's massive success is well-known. Released in 1993, the game's over-the-top gameplay, colorful characters, and witty one-liners captured the hearts of gamers worldwide. 'Boomshakalaka' became an instant catchphrase, symbolizing the game's irreverent humor and charm. However, behind the scenes, Kitzrow's experience was far from glamorous. His $800 payment, a paltry sum considering the game's astronomical earnings, raises important questions about fair compensation for voice actors and the value of their work.

The voice-over industry is built on the talents of skilled actors like Kitzrow, who bring characters and stories to life with their voices. However, the industry's business model often prioritizes profit over fair pay, leaving voice actors vulnerable to exploitation. This is particularly true for those working on high-profile projects, where the financial rewards often go to developers, publishers, and other stakeholders, while the voice actors themselves receive minimal compensation. The 'Boomshakalaka' incident highlights the need for greater transparency and fairness in the voice-over industry, ensuring that voice actors are paid a fair wage for their work.

The impact of this story extends beyond the voice-over industry, resonating with anyone who has ever felt undervalued or underappreciated in their work. It's a powerful reminder that fair compensation is not just a matter of money, but also of respect and recognition. As we celebrate the iconic games and characters that have shaped our culture, we must also acknowledge the talented individuals behind them, who deserve to be paid fairly for their contributions. The 'Boomshakalaka' incident serves as a call to action, encouraging us to reexamine the way we value and compensate voice actors, and to work towards a more equitable and sustainable industry.

In conclusion, the story of Tim Kitzrow and the 'Boomshakalaka' catchphrase is a sobering reminder of the often-exploitative nature of the entertainment industry. As we look to the future of voice-over and content creation, it's essential that we prioritize fairness, transparency, and respect for the talented individuals who bring our favorite characters and stories to life. By doing so, we can create a more just and sustainable industry, where voice actors like Kitzrow are valued and compensated for their incredible work.
