Jujutsu Kaisen Season 3 Casting Sends Shockwaves

Yoshiko Sakakibara joins as Tengen, narrator

Anime News Network
November 19, 2025
3 min read

Imagine being transported to a world of dark fantasy, where sorcerers and cursed spirits collide in a battle for survival. This is the world of Jujutsu Kaisen, a popular anime series that has captured the hearts of millions. According to Anime News Network, the official website for the Jujutsu Kaisen anime has confirmed that Yoshiko Sakakibara will voice the character Tengen in Jujutsu Kaisen Season 3: The Culling Game Part 1, the first part of the highly anticipated new season. Sakakibara will also continue to narrate the series, building on her work from season 2.

The Jujutsu Kaisen series, known for its intricate storyline and complex characters, has been a favorite among anime fans since its debut. The show's unique blend of action, horror, and fantasy elements has made it a standout in the anime world. With the addition of Sakakibara as Tengen, fans are eagerly awaiting the new season, which promises to bring even more excitement and drama to the series.
