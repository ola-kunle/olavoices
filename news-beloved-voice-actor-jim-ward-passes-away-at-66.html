Beloved Voice Actor Jim Ward Passes Away at 66

Legendary voice behind Knights of the Old Republic and more

PC Gamer
December 13, 2025
3 min read

The world of voice acting has lost a shining star. Jim Ward, the prolific voice actor behind iconic characters in Knights of the Old Republic, New Vegas, and Grim Fandango, has passed away at the age of 66. This devastating news has sent shockwaves through the gaming and voice-over communities, leaving fans and fellow actors reeling in grief. According to PC Gamer, Ward's acting career spanned dozens of videogames, including his memorable role as Captain Qwark in Ratchet & Clank.

Ward's impressive body of work is a testament to his talent and dedication to his craft. With a career spanning multiple decades, he brought countless characters to life, leaving an indelible mark on the world of voice acting. His contributions to the industry will be deeply felt, and his legacy will continue to inspire future generations of voice actors. As a radio personality, Ward was also a beloved host and commentator, sharing his wit and charm with audiences beyond the realm of videogames.

The voice-over industry is particularly feeling the impact of Ward's passing. As a seasoned voice actor, he had worked with numerous studios and directors, providing guidance and mentorship to up-and-coming talent. His experience and expertise will be deeply missed, and his absence will be felt in the many projects he had lined up. The voice-over community is known for its camaraderie and support, and Ward's passing serves as a poignant reminder of the importance of nurturing and celebrating the talented individuals who bring characters to life. For voice-over professionals, Ward's legacy is a reminder of the power of their craft, and the impact they can have on audiences around the world.

Ward's passing also highlights the importance of preserving the history and heritage of voice acting. As the industry continues to evolve, it's essential to recognize and honor the contributions of pioneers like Ward, who helped shape the medium into what it is today. By sharing their stories and experiences, we can ensure that their legacy lives on, inspiring new generations of voice actors and content creators. According to PC Gamer, Ward's death has sparked an outpouring of tributes and condolences from fans and fellow actors, a testament to the profound impact he had on the industry and the lives of those who knew him.

As we mourn the loss of this incredible talent, we're reminded of the profound impact voice actors have on our lives. They bring joy, excitement, and emotional depth to the characters we love, and their work has the power to transcend borders, cultures, and generations. Jim Ward's passing is a poignant reminder of the importance of celebrating and supporting the talented individuals who make this magic happen. As we look to the future, we must continue to nurture and elevate the art of voice acting, ensuring that the legacy of pioneers like Ward continues to inspire and uplift us.

In conclusion, the passing of Jim Ward is a devastating loss for the voice-over industry and the world at large. His incredible talent, generosity, and dedication to his craft have left an indelible mark on our lives, and his legacy will continue to inspire and motivate us. As we say goodbye to this beloved voice actor, we're reminded of the transformative power of voice acting and the importance of preserving its history, heritage, and community. Let us honor Jim Ward's memory by continuing to celebrate the art of voice acting and the talented individuals who bring characters to life, one voice at a time.
