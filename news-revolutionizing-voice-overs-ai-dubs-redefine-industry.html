Revolutionizing Voice-Overs: AI Dubs Redefine Industry

81Produce and ElevenLabs partner for AI-driven voice talent

Anime News Network
December 16, 2025
3 min read

Imagine a world where your favorite anime characters' voices are no longer limited to their original actors. A world where AI technology can mimic the tone, pitch, and emotion of a seasoned voice actor, creating a seamless listening experience. This is the future that 81Produce Agency and AI software company ElevenLabs are shaping with their recent partnership. According to Anime News Network, the two companies announced a business partnership on Monday, aimed at creating AI dubs based on voice talent.

The partnership between 81Produce and ElevenLabs is a significant development in the voice-over industry. ElevenLabs will utilize its cutting-edge AI technology, including VoiceCAPCHA, digital watermark, and C2PA standards, to combat unauthorized generative AI use. This collaboration has far-reaching implications for voice actors, voice-over work, and content creation as a whole. With the ability to create AI-generated voice-overs that are almost indistinguishable from the real thing, the industry is poised for a revolution.

For voice-over professionals, this partnership raises both opportunities and challenges. On one hand, AI-generated voice-overs could open up new avenues for voice actors to showcase their talent, allowing them to focus on more creative aspects of their work. On the other hand, the increasing use of AI in voice-overs may lead to concerns about job security and the potential for AI to replace human voice actors. As the industry continues to evolve, it is essential for voice-over professionals to stay adaptable and open to the changing landscape.

The impact of this partnership extends beyond the voice-over industry, with significant emotional and social implications. For fans of anime and other forms of voice-over content, the ability to experience their favorite characters in new and innovative ways can be a game-changer. Imagine being able to enjoy your favorite anime series in a language you're not fluent in, with AI-generated voice-overs that capture the nuances and emotions of the original performance. This technology has the potential to break down language barriers and bring people closer together through shared cultural experiences.

As we move forward in this new era of AI-driven voice-overs, it is crucial to consider the potential consequences of this technology. While it offers many benefits, such as increased accessibility and creative possibilities, it also raises important questions about the role of human voice actors in the industry. As we continue to push the boundaries of what is possible with AI, we must prioritize the well-being and livelihoods of the talented individuals who bring characters to life with their voices.

In conclusion, the partnership between 81Produce and ElevenLabs marks a significant turning point in the voice-over industry. As we embark on this new journey, it is essential to recognize the potential of AI technology to revolutionize the way we experience voice-overs. With its ability to mimic human emotion and create seamless listening experiences, AI has the power to bring people closer together and transcend language barriers. As we look to the future, one thing is clear: the voice-over industry will never be the same again.
