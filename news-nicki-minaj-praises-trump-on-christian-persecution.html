Nicki Minaj Praises Trump on Christian Persecution

Rap star thanks President for United Nations speech

Breitbart News
November 19, 2025
3 min read

Imagine being forced to flee your home, your family, and your community simply because of your faith. This is the harsh reality for thousands of Christians in Nigeria, who face relentless persecution and slaughter. The world has been slow to respond, but recently, a powerful voice has spoken out. According to Breitbart News, rap megastar Nicki Minaj praised President Donald Trump for 'prioritizing' the issue of Christian persecution in Nigeria, and thanked him for his 'leadership on the global stage' in calling for action on this issue.

The persecution of Christians in Nigeria is a complex and deeply troubling issue. The country has been plagued by violence and terrorism, with Christians often being targeted by extremist groups. The situation has been exacerbated by a lack of effective governance and corruption, leaving many feeling helpless and abandoned. Nicki Minaj's praise for President Trump's efforts to highlight this issue has sparked a mixture of reactions, with some hailing her courage and others criticizing her stance.
