Uncovered: The Lost Troy McClure Movie Pitch

A glimpse into a forgotten 'Simpsons' film project

Cracked.com
December 7, 2025
3 min read

Imagine a world where Troy McClure, the lovable, over-the-top character from 'The Simpsons,' had his own live-action movie. It's a concept that's both fascinating and heartbreaking, especially considering the talent behind the character, Phil Hartman, is no longer with us. According to Cracked.com, Hartman had pitched a live-action Troy McClure movie to the 'Simpsons' writers, which would have been the first 'Simpsons' feature film. This revelation not only gives us a glimpse into a forgotten project but also highlights the enduring legacy of Phil Hartman's work.

The idea of a Troy McClure movie is intriguing, to say the least. For those who may not be familiar, Troy McClure is a fictional character known for his outrageous educational films, such as 'The President's Neck is Missing,' 'The Erotic Adventures of Hercules,' and 'Fuzzy Bunny's Guide to You-Know-What.' These films, often featured in 'The Simpsons,' have become a staple of the show's humor, showcasing Hartman's incredible voice acting range and comedic timing.

Phil Hartman's contribution to 'The Simpsons' cannot be overstated. His voice acting brought to life not only Troy McClure but also Lionel Hutz, a bumbling, corrupt lawyer, among other characters. Hartman's talent and dedication to his craft have inspired countless voice actors and continue to influence the voice-over industry.

The voice-over industry is where this story takes an interesting turn. The potential for a live-action Troy McClure movie, had it been made, would have opened up new avenues for voice-over work and content creation. Voice actors could have explored the possibility of transitioning from animation to live-action, using their versatile voices to bring characters to life in a different medium. Moreover, the success of such a film could have paved the way for more live-action adaptations of animated characters, creating new opportunities for voice-over professionals. This not only affects voice actors but also the broader scope of content creation, as it challenges traditional boundaries between animation and live-action.

The emotional and social impact of this story lies in the 'what if' scenario it presents. What if Phil Hartman's pitch had been accepted? What if Troy McClure had made it to the big screen in a live-action film? It's a reminder of the power of creativity and the importance of taking risks in the entertainment industry. It also underscores the lasting impact of Phil Hartman's work and the characters he helped bring to life.

In conclusion, the story of Phil Hartman's pitch for a live-action Troy McClure movie is a poignant reminder of the innovative spirit that drives the entertainment industry. For voice-over professionals, it's a call to explore new horizons, just as Hartman did with his versatile voice acting. As we look back on what could have been, we're also reminded of the incredible talent that has shaped the voice-over industry and the endless possibilities that lie ahead for those who dare to dream big.
