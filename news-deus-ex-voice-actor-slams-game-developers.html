Deus Ex Voice Actor Slams Game Developers

Elias Toufexis speaks out against Embracer Group

Wccftech
January 2, 2026
3 min read

Imagine being the voice behind a beloved character, only to see the franchise you've grown to love be left in limbo. That's the harsh reality facing Elias Toufexis, the voice actor behind Adam Jensen from the Deus Ex series. According to Wccftech, Toufexis recently expressed his frustration with the lack of a new Deus Ex game, bluntly stating that 'the people in charge are psychopaths.' These strong words have sent shockwaves through the gaming community, leaving fans and voice-over professionals alike wondering what's next for the iconic series.

The Deus Ex series has been a staple in the gaming world for decades, with its thought-provoking storylines and immersive gameplay. As the voice of Adam Jensen, Toufexis has been an integral part of the franchise's success. However, with no new game on the horizon for 2026, it's clear that something is amiss. The Embracer Group, the company behind the Deus Ex series, has been tight-lipped about their plans for the franchise, leaving fans and voice actors like Toufexis in the dark.

For voice-over professionals, this situation highlights the often-unstable nature of the industry. One day, you're working on a high-profile project; the next, it's put on indefinite hold. This unpredictability can be unsettling, to say the least. The voice-over industry is built on creativity and passion, but it's also a business. When games like Deus Ex are left in limbo, it not only affects the voice actors involved but also the entire ecosystem of content creators who rely on these projects for work. This can lead to a ripple effect, impacting studios, directors, and other professionals who bring these games to life.

The lack of a new Deus Ex game also raises questions about the cultural impact of the series. The Deus Ex games have always been known for their thought-provoking themes and social commentary. By leaving the series dormant, the Embracer Group may be missing an opportunity to tap into the current cultural zeitgeist. The gaming community is hungry for games that challenge their perspectives and spark meaningful conversations. The Deus Ex series has always been at the forefront of this type of storytelling, and its absence is deeply felt.

As the gaming industry continues to evolve, it's essential to consider the human side of game development. Voice actors like Elias Toufexis are not just talented professionals; they're also passionate fans who have invested their hearts and souls into these projects.
